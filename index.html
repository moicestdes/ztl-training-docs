Program Features

Here is an overview of features to expect upon initial release!

Voice Recognition A.I.

To truly capture the essence of the Vatsim environment, this software comes with A.I. pilots who will make calls for clearances, taxi, readback commands, and correct the user on issues.

Full Support for Ground and Clearance Delivery

Upon initial release, the program will allow users to work both ground and clearance delivery positions. All roles above these positions will be fully automated until future updates.

Windows and Mac-Friendly

In consideration of the range of devices used to connect to Vatsim's network, this program is designed with both windows and mac users in mind!

Designed for Practical Training

This program is designed to provide trainees with the preparation needed for running sweatboxes during training sessions. Success in practical sessions are beneficial to both trainees and their mentors!
